Scratchpad Upgrade to Drupal 6 Status Document

The following table provides a list of modules currently in use on the Scratchpads (including their current usage), and their porting to Drupal 6 status (Extra information). Note, modules developed by S.D.Rycroft which require porting are marked as "Fine" whilst those developed by B.Scott are marked as a "Problem", purely given the fact that Ben is only with us until April.

I've attempted to categorise the modules into three:

  • Modules which are either ported, or no longer needed
  • Modules which are not ported, but shouldn't stop us from upgrading
  • Modules which are not ported, and that the Scratchpad's rely on. Without attention these will stop a Drupal upgrade
