Create a forum and a forum container (only maintainers):

  • Go to the advanced administration page by clicking on 'Administer' and then the 'Advanced' link in the Navigation menu and click on 'Forums' in the content management section.
  • To add a new forum click on 'Add Forum', enter the name of the forum a description and a parent term if you want a hierarchy of forums. Save.
  • You can also add a container to organize your forums better. See the help text on the forum administration pages for more information.

You can access the forum by clicking on ‘Forum topic’ in the ‘Content’ menu.
