• Go to ‘Administer’ , 'Advanced', ‘Content management’ and
    ‘Content types'.
  • Click on ‘Export’, choose the content type you want to clone and click on ‘submit’.
  • Choose the groups and fields you want to copy and submit.
  • Copy all the text in the textbox (Ctrl+a, Ctrl+c).
  • Add a new content type (just enter ‘name’ and
    ‘type’ and save).
  • Click on ‘Import’, choose your new content type, copy the text into the textbox (Ctrl+v) and submit.

If you only want to import you just need to follow the last two points. If you only want to export you need to follow the first four points and then save the text as a txt file.
