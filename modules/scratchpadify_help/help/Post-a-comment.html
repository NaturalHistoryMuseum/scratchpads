Every node (e.g. page, biblio etc) that allows comments ends with a link ‘Add new comment’. Click on this link and enter the subject of your comment and the comment itself. If the ‘Comment’ field shows as a white square you either have to enter plain text, text in html code, or you can click on ‘enable rich text’. In the rich text editor you can enter and format your text similar to using MS Word.

Click on ‘Preview comment’, edit the text if necessary and click on ‘Post comment’.
