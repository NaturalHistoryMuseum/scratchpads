• Download the "Carnidae_ITIS_Taxonomy.txt" spreadsheet.
  • Open the tab-delimited file in Excel. You will see a Parent-Child way of depicting the classification, which follows the ITIS (Integrated Taxonomic Information System) standard. Close the file without saving. Then repeat steps 1-2 above using a new classification name (e.g. "Carnidae_ITIS") and under 'Settings' selecting 'ITIS Name' in the 'Content type' drop down menu at the bottom.
  • Click the 'Import' tab [not the 'Import classification' tab!] and then on the new vocabulary you just created.
  • Extend the 'CSV File' section, browse for the " Carnidae_ITIS_Taxonomy.txt" file and select the 'ITIS Parent/Child' file type.
  • Click on the 'Import from CSV File' button and wait until the TinyTax block appears in the left sidebar.
  • Check out the difference that the connection with the 'ITIS Name' content type makes in the taxonomic editor. Click on 'Taxonomy' in the Content menu, find the new ITIS classification and click on the 'Editor' link. Explore the form on the right side, which now shows much more tabs and fields.
