Help and tips for working with Scratchpads can be found in a series of help pages available through clicking on the questionmark icons. Screencasts are available at http://scratchpads.eu/screencasts. The screencasts have not yet been updated to the current Drupal 6 version of the Scratchpads and will deviate from what you can actually see and do in some cases.

For feature requests and bug reports please use the Issues button which appears on the right hand side when you are logged in. If you have specific questions or would like advice or help in how to best set up your site, please email the Scratchpad team at scratchpad@nhm.ac.uk. Don't be afraid to ask 'stupid' questions and don't get hung up on a problem for hours.
