• Click on the 'Add a Page Node' icon which appears when you hover over the 'Page' link in the 'Content' menu on the left side of your site.
  • We need an introductory page for the Carnidae, so enter a 'Title', e.g. "Introduction to Carnidae", and some 'Body' text for your page and click the 'Save' button. You have just created your first page.
  • Edit content on the page by clicking the 'Edit' tab. The 'Body' section has a rich text editor. Explore some of the features of the rich text editor by modifying the format of the text you have entered.
  • Note the publishing options available under 'Show Advanced Options' toward the bottom of the page. These options are available to most types of content published in a Scratchpad.
  • Click the 'Save' button once you have completed the page.
  • Click the 'Page' link in the Content menu to find the page you just made. Click on the link (in the title) to view the page.
