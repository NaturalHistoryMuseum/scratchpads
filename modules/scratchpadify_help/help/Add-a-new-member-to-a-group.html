Minimal user role: group manager.

There are two options:

  • Click 'Group' in the Content menu and click on the name of the group in the table. In the Content menu underneath the 'Group' link will now be two additional links to "Members" and "My membership". Click on "Members" and then on the 'Add members' tab to add users.
  • Or go to a page in your group. Click on ‘1 member’ (or 2, 3, ... members) in the Group details block in the left or right sidebar. Click on "Add member" and enter the user name of the new member. If you don't see the Group details block, you have to activate it on the 'Blocks' administration page.
