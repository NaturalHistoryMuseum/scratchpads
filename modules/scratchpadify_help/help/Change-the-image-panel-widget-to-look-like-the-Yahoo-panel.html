• Go to a taxon page for which you have several images.
  • Hover with the mouse over the content in the 'Image' panel and click on the 'Edit' link, which appears in small grey writing underneath the panel title. You can now edit the view for this panel. However, you need to be aware that if there are any general changes to the image module rolled out by us, your image panel might not show properly any more. If this is the case you have to click the 'Revert' button at the bottom and if necessary readjust the view.
  • To change the image panel from showing a list of image nodes to showing a grid of images go to the 'Basic settings' and click on Style: 'Unformatted', select 'Grid' and click the 'Update button.
  • Still under the 'Basic settings' click on Row style: 'Node', select 'Fields' and click the 'Update button.
  • Under 'Fields' click on the plus icon. Select 'Content' in the 'Groups' dropdown menu and select 'Content: Image (field_imagefield)'. Click the 'Add' button.
  • At the bottom select 'None' under 'Label' and in the 'Format' dropdown menu select 'Lichtbox2: thumbnail_square->preview' and click the 'Update button.
  • Click the 'Save' button.
