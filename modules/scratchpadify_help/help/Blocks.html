A block is a box of content that may be rendered into certain regions of your web pages, for example, into sidebars. Frequently a block contains a menu.

  • Go to ‘Administer’ > 'Layout’ > ‘Blocks’.
  • If you want to add a block click on ‘Add block’, fill in the requested information and save the block.
  • To make the new or an existing disabled block active you have to give it a place on the page by clicking on <none> under ‘Regions’ and choosing one of the places. The block will move to the place you indicated. You can also drag & drop the block at the correct position.
  • Click on ‘Save blocks’. Click on ‘configure’ to change block specific settings.
  • If you want the block to appear only within a certain group go to ‘Page specific visibility settings’, check ‘Show on only the listed pages’ and enter the pages. For one group you have to enter “content/groupname” as well as “groupname/*” in the next line to cover all the pages within this group.
