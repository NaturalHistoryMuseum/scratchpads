• Wait for the Java applet to load and click “trust” to confirm that you agree to use the applet.
  • Drag images from your PC onto the applet once it has loaded or click on 'Add files' and browse for your images (see screenshot).
  • Click on 'Start upload'.
  • After the images have uploaded, click on 'Edit uploaded images' to annotate several images at once using the matrix editor.
