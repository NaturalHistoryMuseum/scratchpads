• Click on 'Administer' > 'Advanced' in your user menu and click on 'GMap' under the site configuration section.
  • Under 'Default width' you should enter "100%". This will ensure that the map fills the screen in an optimal way without overlapping over the side bars.
  • Under 'Default map type' you can choose whether you want the street, satellite or hybrid map showing as default.
  • Under 'Map behaviour flags' it can be useful to check 'Autozoom', which will automatically zoom the map to fit all markers when markers are added.

You can change many more options. Be aware that every change you make in the GMap module will effect all the Google maps on your site, including the specimen map on the taxon pages and any custom maps you might have made.
