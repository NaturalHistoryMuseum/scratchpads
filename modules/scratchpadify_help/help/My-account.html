You can personalise your Scratchpad account and change the settings.

  • Go to the Navigation menu (the one under your user name) in the left or right sidebar and click on ‘My account’ and ‘Edit’.
  • Under ‘Account’ you can change your password and other settings. For example under ‘Biblio settings’ you can choose to show your publications on your profile page if you link it to the correct author from the Biblio database.
    Under ‘Location’ you can enter your location by clicking on the map. However, this map will not be visible, currently.
  • Under ‘Profile' you can change you personal data.
  • Under ‘My newsletter’ you can subscribe to newsletters.
  • You can also add OpenID URLs under ‘OpenID Identities’, view your posts to this Scratchpad under ‘Track’ or upload files to your 'File browser'.
