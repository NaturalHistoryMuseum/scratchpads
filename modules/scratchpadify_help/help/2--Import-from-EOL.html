• Click the 'Import classification' tab [not the 'Import' tab!] to reveal the options for importing a classification. If you have more than one vocabulary, you need to select the new vocabulary you just created (Carnidae) under the 'Destination Classification' pull down menu. Note that you will not need to select a taxonomy if there is only one (e.g. the Carnidae one that you have just added).
  • Click the 'Import from EOL' link and enter "Carnidae" in the text field.
  • Click search. The name "Carnidae" appears in several possible classifications. Choose a Species 2000 classification, and click 'Import Classification'.

This will import the complete classification of the taxon you type, into the Scratchpad. We recommend that you restrict your choice to groups that contain less than 50,000 taxa. When the import is complete, a TinyTax block with the classification will appear in the top left sidebar of your site. Refresh your browser window if you don't see this block.

  • To update your classification click on 'Taxonomy' in the Content menu, find the Carnidae classification and click on the 'Editor' link. Explore the taxonomic editor and all of its functions.
