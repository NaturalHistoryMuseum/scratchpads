After logging in for the first time, each user has so agree to the terms and conditions of a Scratchpad. These terms and conditions are written to reflect mainly the relationship between the maintainer and other users of the Scratchpad and the provider of the Scratchpad template (EDIT).

You can change the text of the terms and conditions to reflect the relationship between maintainer and other users if you want.

  • Go to ‘Administer’ > 'Advanced' > ‘Site configuration > ‘Legal’.
  • You can change the text (html) in the text box under ‘Terms & Conditions’ and add additional checkboxes if needed.
