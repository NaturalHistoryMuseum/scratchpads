1. Click on the 'Add a Page Node' icon which appears when you hover over the 'Page' link in the 'Content' menu on the left side of your site.
  2. We need an introductory page for the Carnidae, so enter a 'Title', e.g. "Introduction to Carnidae", and some 'Body' text for your page and click the 'Save' button. You have just created your first page.
  3. Edit content on the page by clicking the 'Edit' tab. The 'Body' section has a rich text editor. Explore some of the features of the rich text editor by modifying the format of the text you have entered.
  4. Note the publishing options available under 'Show Advanced Options' toward the bottom of the page. These options are available to most types of content published in a Scratchpad.
  5. Click the 'Save' button once you have completed the page.
  6. Click the 'Page' link in the Content menu to find the page you just made. Click on the link (in the title) to view the page.
