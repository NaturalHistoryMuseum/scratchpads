Go to ‘User login’ in the left or right sidebar, click on ‘Create new account’ and enter the requested data. For some Scratchpads there will be the option to subscribe to one or more of the groups. If you want to do this go to ‘Groups’ and check the box next to the group you want to subscribe to. Check ‘Accept Terms & Conditions of Use’ and click on ‘Create new account’.

For Scratchpads as well as for groups where approval is needed, the manager will receive an email with your request and will then decide on your subscription. Depending on the settings of the site you may receive a confirmation email.
