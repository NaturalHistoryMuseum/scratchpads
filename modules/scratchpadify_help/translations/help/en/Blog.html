A blog is a webpage with regular entries by one or more persons writing on one or more topics.

  • To add a blog entry, enter a title and text for your blog entry, tag your blog to the classification if necessary and save.
  • To reply to a blog entry click on 'Add new comment', to quote an entry click on ‘Quote’.
  • To see other blog entries from the same user click on ‘xxx’s blog’.
  • Usually only a teaser, that is the first paragraph of a blog entry will be shown on the blog's main page. To read the full entry click on 'Read more'.
