• Go to the advanced administration page by clicking on 'Administration' and then the 'Advanced' link in the Navigation menu and click on 'Forums' in the content management section.
  • To add a new forum click on 'Add Forum', enter the name of the forum a description and a parent term if you want a hierarchy of forums. Save.
  • You can also add a container to organize your forums better. Click on the 'more help' link for further information.
