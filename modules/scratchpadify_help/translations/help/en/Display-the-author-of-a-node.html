• Go to the advanced administration page by clicking on 'Administer' and then 'Advanced' in the navigation menu under your user name. Under ‘Site building’ click on ‘Themes’.
  • Now click on ‘Configure’ at the top of the themes page.
  • Under ‘Display post information on’ choose the types of post which should display the username of the person who submitted this post and the date. This will stay the same even when the node is updated. The post information will appear between the title and the text of the post in the format: "Mon, 2008-06-09 08:42 — username"
  • Click on ‘Save configuration’.
