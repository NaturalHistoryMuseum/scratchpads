To create an image gallery:

  • Click on the 'Add a Image node' icon in the 'Content' menu and then click on 'Add a new image Gallery' under the Image gallery drop down box.
  • Give the gallery a name and description. Under the 'Advanced options' you can choose a parent image gallery or “weight” the gallery to determine its position relative to other image galleries – the default gallery is simply called “Library”. When complete, click 'Save'.
  • When you click on the image gallery page …/image you will see a thumbnail of the last image uploaded as an example for this gallery. If you want to fix a specific image in this place you need to edit this image and select 'Sticky at top of lists' under the 'Publishing options'.

Image galleries are based on an 'Image gallery' taxonomy. Therefore to delete a gallery or edit the name, you need to delete/edit the respective term in this taxonomy:

  • Click on 'Taxonomy' in the Content menu
  • Click on 'list terms' for the 'Image galleries' taxonomy.
  • Click on 'edit' for the term you want to delete or edit.
  • Either click on the 'Delete' button or edit the term and click the 'Save' button.
