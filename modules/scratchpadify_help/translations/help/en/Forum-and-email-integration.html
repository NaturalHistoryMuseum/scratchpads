You can integrate a forum with your email, so that you will receive emails about new forum posts and can reply to these via your email (authenticated users):

  • Go to 'My account' in the Navigation menu and click on the 'Forum/E-mail integration' tab.
  • Select the forums you want to integrate with your email and click the 'Subscribe' button.
  • You can also add additional email addresses from which you will be able to send emails to the forum. However, you will not receive forum emails on this address.
