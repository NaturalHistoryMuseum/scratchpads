The Autotag module enables the quick and easy tagging of a node with the tags it is most likely associated with.

The module searches the content of a node, and compares it against the tags in the vocabularies associated with that node's content type. It also checks against the synonyms of those terms.

Only Vocabularies marked as being multiple select will be used, and those that are marked as required will not be (as Autotag would prevent the submission of the form).

The Vocabularies that will be searched against are marked with a "†", any that are not marked will not be searched.
