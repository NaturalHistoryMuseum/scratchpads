• Go to the advanced administration page by clicking on 'Administer' and then 'Advanced' in the navigation menu under your user name. Under ‘Site building’ click on ‘Themes’.
  • Now click on ‘Configure’ at the top of the themes page.
  • Under ‘Display post information on’ choose the types of post which should display the username of the person who submitted this post and the date. Once the node has been edited this will change to the username of the person who edited the node and the date this happend. The post information will appear between the title and the text of the post in the format: "Submitted/Edited by username on Mon, 2008-06-09 08:42"
  • Click on ‘Save configuration’.
